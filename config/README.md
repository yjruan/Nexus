I compared the calculation results obtained from the [Z1+ code](https://github.com/mkmat/Z1plus-code/) and FT-PPA. 
The `startup`, `steady`, and `snapshot` directories correspond to selected results related to Figures 3–5 in the manuscript, respectively.
Notably, the data are ensemble-averaged results in the manuscript, whereas the result provided here is based on an instantaneous configurations. 
Therefore, some quantitative differences between the two sets of results are expected.
